Why we built this

AI-generated content is spreading faster than ever — fake voices, fake faces, fake news. Everyone is at risk, but older adults are targeted most often by sophisticated scams.

This tool was built on a simple belief: everyone, regardless of age or tech skills, deserves to navigate the internet safely.

This tool is AI-assisted and not infallible. When in doubt, always check with a trusted person or an official source directly.
